Our Story

Rio Alto brings recipes from Mexico City and nearby provinces, cooked in a traditional Mexican furnace you won’t find anywhere else. We use local Alberta beef (90% lean) and vegetables from nearby farms, and never use canned or preprocessed ingredients. Small batch. Big flavor. Always authentic.
